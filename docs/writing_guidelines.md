# Writing guidelines

- I write posts in the style of casual expertise, like a friendly and
  talented teacher explaining complex topics with simple language and examples.
- I use simple language. Not "flowery" or overly dramatic, but still compelling
  and easy to read.
- My writing is more like an entertaining conference talk and less like a business whitepaper.
- I avoid typical AI writing cliches like "not just X, but Y" and "the
  real... is..." because they can be overused and sound cliche. Instead, I prefer
  to make clear and direct statements that focus on the specific benefits and
  features of the project.
- I do not use em-dashes or en-dashes.
- Prefer Sentence case for headings and subheadings
